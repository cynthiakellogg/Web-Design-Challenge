Project Summary

The team wanted to focus on something that would bring us joy. We decided to work with the world happieness dataset. We spent 10 days analyizing the factors of happiness provided in the study as well as brainstorming some potential external factors that might influence happiness.

Here are the 6 factors used to measure happiness:
Income (GDP per Capita) (data from World Bank)
Healthy life expectancy (data from WHO, WDI)
Freedom
Trust (in government / business)
Generosity
Social support

My specific role was to look at possible other events or factors that might influnce world happiness that are not accounted for in this study. After a bit of a brainstorm with the team we decided to look at to major world events:

  1. The 2018 World Cup, Hosted in Russia
  2. The 2018 Winter Olympics, Hosted in South Korea

The hypothesis was that 2018 would have higher scores than the rest of the years in the data set due to 2 major world events that are designed to bring people together for fun.
