Project Summary

The team wanted to focus on something that would bring us joy. We decided to work with the world happieness dataset. My role was to look at possible other events or factors that might influnce world happiness that are not accounted for in this study. After a team brainstorm, we decided to look at 2 major world events:

  1. The 2018 World Cup, Hosted in Russia
  2. The 2018 Winter Olympics, Hosted in South Korea

The hypothesis was that 2018 would have higher scores than the rest of the years in the data set due to 2 major world events that are designed to bring people together for fun. I joined all the CSV files for each year, did some data cleaning so that each years data matched, and then generated a way to input one country at a time to look at it's scores over the 5 years. Additionally, I calculated summary statistics for all the countries for each year to pull out the mean. I was looking to see what the trends were for individual coutnries as well as the world as a whole. Dive into the comparisons page to see what we learned.
